00Compatibility verdict

deeply compatible3 hard gates The two systems are convergent evolution from the same instinct: a 64-bit packet as the atom of verifiable state, an untrusted proposer split from an exact certifier, and honesty as an engineering discipline. Our POLYCENTRIA.md dual-helix (oracle proposes / certifier decides) and their six-Point topology describe the same governance shape at two altitudes. Conversion is therefore mostly a mapping, not a rewrite — but three gates must clear first (§05): the naming collision, the IP-assignment chain for the pump kernel, and public-repo disclosure hygiene.

Polycentria (Emotivity Inc.)

The rhyme is not cosmetic: both stacks refuse a privileged center (their "composition privileges no participant" ↔ our "no node trusts another's verdict"), both measure instead of asserting, and both put the checkpoint before the action (their Point of Control ↔ our crystallization windows).

05Compatibility gates — the troubleshoot, honestly

GATE 1 · The naming collision. "Polycentria" is simultaneously our governance model (the Polycentria Dual-Helix, Library/Experimental_Systems/POLYCENTRIA.md) and their organization (Emotivity Inc., dba Polycentria). Worse, the two document sets contradict each other on Schism Labs: our POLYCENTRIA.md says "the organization name Schism Labs is unchanged," their staged content says "the Schism Labs second entity is deprecated — do not reintroduce." Resolution needed before any shared publication: one owner for each name, one sentence recording the relationship. Proposed: the model keeps "Polycentria Dual-Helix" with an attribution line; the org is always "Polycentria (Emotivity Inc.)". decision, not code
GATE 2 · The IP assignment chain for the pump. The Hyperbolic Sparsemax Pump — the executable kernel this whole BioChain deployment uses as its consensus clock — originates here ("BioChain AI · Owl Academy — Experimental Systems") and is simultaneously tracked in their internal new-matter inventory pending an assignment-chain confirmation to Emotivity Inc. Until that chain is confirmed in writing, merging code between the repos is a legal event, not a git event. Everything in this study is mapping and interface design; no code crosses until the assignment question is resolved by counsel. legal gate
GATE 3 · Disclosure hygiene. This repo serves public GitHub Pages; two of the four reviewed documents are internal (one legal-confidential). Rule adopted for this page and everything after it: public pages cite public concepts only — no claim inventories, no filing specifics, no counsel or partner names, no internal roadmap items. Their own discipline ("disclosure to partners is a patent-disclosure event") applies to us the moment we touch shared material. Also worth their attention in the other direction: the pump proof has been publicly visible in this repo's Experimental Systems — the already-public boundary should be inventoried by counsel too. process gate

06Implementation steps

PHASE 0 · gates
Clear the three gates. Naming decision recorded in both document sets · assignment chain for the pump confirmed by counsel before any code crosses · disclosure rule (public pages ↔ public concepts) adopted in writing. Nothing else starts until these do. decision + legal
PHASE 1 · vocabulary
Convert the description layer. Tag every BioChain Enterprise claim with honesty tags · adopt the six Points as section headers in the spec (each mesh mechanism filed under its Point, per the §02 map) · add the Points checklist to the review process · publish the joint conformance contract (their 51 vectors + our golden hashes) as one document. docs only — zero risk
PHASE 2 · measure
One real stream, three experiments. Ingest EuRoC MH_01 through the engram codec (our first real-data boundary) · run the residual channel on their 12/36 gate-missing windows — target: 100% of windows clear SW<0.20 losslessly, with the residual cost priced honestly · add SW(H₁) as the third crystallization score on our side. All three are benchable without any code crossing repos: the stream is public, the methods are papers. [TO-SUBSTANTIATE] → [IN-CODE]
PHASE 3 · interfaces
One wire, two dialects. Reserve the Structural Form ID for Polycentria packets · adopt their adapter contract for engram ingestion (Relation Point) · exchange signed SW-gate verdicts as mesh records (their TDA stays their-side; we verify signatures + thresholds). after gates clear
PHASE 4 · governance merge
Mechanize Reconciliation. Chiral holonomy commits beside their monoid pooling · pairing rule + dual anchors offered as the verifier-network discipline · pump-clock windows as the swarm synchronization substrate · crystallization = their Point of Control bound to tick-6/12. the actual conversion
PHASE 5 · economy
Calibration · Compression · Compliance on the two-token rail. FLUX meters verification work across both networks; CRYST backs compute redemption; value × ε × SW becomes the three-axis crystallization score. [ASPIRATIONAL]
